What we build

One team, from
foundation to keys

Every build is bespoke — any size, shape or finish you want can be achieved, from the finest Canadian western red cedar and Siberian larch through to Hardie plank and the best composite cladding available. Firestone rubber roofing and Osmo oil treatment on the timber as standard. These are the rooms people ask us for most.

Home office & studio

A properly insulated workspace at the bottom of the garden — heated, wired, double glazed and quiet enough to take the call in. Laminate or engineered floors, T&G or plastered internal finish.

Garden gym

Rubber-matted, brightly lit and built to take the weight. Reinforced flooring, full power for cardio equipment, and ventilation that keeps it usable in August.

Garden bar & snug

Bars, snugs and games rooms — bi-folds opening onto the deck, LED lighting, slatted acoustic walls, and room for the dartboard and the beer fridge.

Annexe & shower room

Guest annexes and beauty rooms with fully plumbed shower rooms — tiled, heated and finished to the same standard as the main room.

Why Moorland

Finished like
part of the house

We're an experienced bespoke design and build service. There's no catalogue to pick from and no fixed kit — any size, shape and finish you desire can be achieved, inside and out. What you get isn't a shed with a plug socket. It's a room you'd be happy to open a door onto.

  • Finished, not just built. Quality T&G internal cladding or plastered walls, laminate flooring, internal doors and skirting — decorated and ready to use.
  • Warm all year. Fully insulated floor, walls and roof, uPVC double glazed units, and heating as standard — usable in February, not just July.
  • Wired properly. Full electrics package — sockets where you actually want them, lighting, and heating. Divide the space into separate rooms if you need to.
  • Turn-key, nationwide. Base, build, electrics, interior finish and clean-up — one team, no trades to chase. Based in Colne, fitting across the country.
